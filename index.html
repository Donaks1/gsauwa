About Us

Established in 2021, the Greek Students’ Association of UWA (GSA) was founded with a deep sense of purpose: to bring together young Greeks and Philhellenes who share a passion for Greek culture, language, food, and traditions. Our mission is to provide more than just a student organization; we offer a home where young Greeks can be proud of their identity, deepen their connection to their culture, and form lifelong friendships. We are committed to ensuring that Greek culture not only survives but thrives, remaining an integral part of the lives of young Greeks in Perth.
